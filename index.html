Introduction

Pathlight is a discipleship journey designed to help you grow in your relationship with Jesus Christ by walking in the light of His truth.

This workbook will guide you step by step to understand who Jesus is, what it means to follow Him, and how to live a life that honors God.

Pathlight is meant for people who are just starting their faith journey or are seeking to go deeper in their journey with Him.

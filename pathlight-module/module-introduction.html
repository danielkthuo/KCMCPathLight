Pathlight Discipleship Curriculum

Lit to Lead. Rooted to Grow.

A comprehensive discipleship program designed to ground new believers in foundational Christian principles and equip them for a life of faithful service.

Program Overview

The Pathlight Discipleship Curriculum is a structured program that guides new believers through essential Christian teachings and practices. Through five comprehensive lessons, participants will build a solid foundation for their faith journey, learning to follow Jesus in every area of life.

Program Objectives

Establish Biblical Foundation

Ground new believers in essential Christian doctrines and practices

Develop Spiritual Disciplines

Cultivate habits of prayer, Bible study, fellowship, and worship

Equip for Service

Prepare believers to use their spiritual gifts and share their faith

Build Spiritual Resilience

Strengthen believers to stand firm in spiritual battles

Getting Started

To begin your discipleship journey with the Pathlight Curriculum:

  1. Start with Lesson 1 to build a solid foundation
  2. Complete each lesson in sequence for optimal learning
  3. Engage with all worksheets and reflection activities
  4. Find an accountability partner or small group to journey with
  5. Apply what you're learning in daily life

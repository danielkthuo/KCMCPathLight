Introduction

PathLight is a discipleship journey designed to help you grow in your relationship with Jesus Christ by walking in the light of His truth. It draws inspiration from Psalm 119:105 — “Your word is a lamp to my feet, and a light to my path.”

This workbook will guide you step by step to understand who Jesus is, what it means to follow Him, and how to live a life that honors God. As part of this journey, you will learn important truths from the Bible (doctrine), enrich your prayer life, discover your identity in Christ, grow in God's character, and serve others with your gifts.

PathLight is for anyone beginning or deepening their faith journey. You will gather in sessions, reflect on Scripture, and participate in activities that strengthen your faith community and your personal walk with God.

Strategic Plan

The Strategic Plan for CY Ministry is a framework to raise young people in the way of Jesus through four key pillars: Evangelism, Discipleship, Life Skills, and Leadership Development. It helps refocus efforts toward intentional spiritual growth and purpose-driven ministry.

Our mission is to redirect young people back to their Creator amidst distractions of modern life and spread the Gospel to all ages. The four pillars form the foundation of the CY Ministry strategy, guiding all actions toward our ultimate purpose — Christ-centered transformation.

Children's Ministry Mission Statement

“To see children & youths come to know Christ and be Biblically discipled; to consistently grow in Christ and become fulfilled Christian adults.”

Proverbs 22:6 — “Train up a child in the way he should go and when he is old, he will not depart from it.”

Children's Ministry Vision Statement

“To reach into the lives of children & youth in our church and community with the Good News, portray the love of Christ, and teach them the Word of God.”

We count it a privilege and responsibility to reach and teach these little ones for Him.

Children's Ministry Core Values

  • CHRIST-CENTERED: Promoting a Christ-centered focus in a “me-centered” culture.
  • LOVE: Valuing each child and encouraging mutual respect and love.
  • SERVING OTHERS: Encouraging children to serve in their families, schools, church, and community.
  • SCRIPTURE: Providing a solid Biblical foundation and teaching children to think deeply about God's Word.
  • PRAYER: Teaching and modeling prayer so children grow to know, trust, and serve Jesus.
  • EXCELLENCE: Striving for excellence in all things for the glory of God.
  • THE MISSION: Inspiring children to make disciples by praying for and sharing Jesus with others.
